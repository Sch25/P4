Story Overview

The creatures deemed monsters by humanity, are in constant conflict against humanity's ire. A grey dragon from a time when humanity and the monsters coexisted sends his grandchild to find the truth behind the start of the conflict. Traveling through the heart of Treespass the land between the safety of home and the might of humanity's march, the old dragon's grandchild called Little Wing by his companions delves through danger and mystery discovering the world and hunting the truth between the conflict. "If we could coexist in the past why not now?" he wonders. With his childhood friend Deeyu, an aeoserpouse (large winged snake-mouse), and his grandfathers old friend that few know more than her name, Alairpha a thorny fox dragon, he sets out for ruins deep within human territory where the rift between humans and the rest of the inhabitants of Terrdivaeros began. With Alairpha as their guide they hope to slip through Tresspass and find the ruins without finding themselves as quarry, and possibly undermining their attempt to resolve the conflict.
