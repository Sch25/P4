The Development
Dragmeria is a Comic written and drawn by Steven Holmes during what free time. Its a side project to work on his art and story telling skills while a larger project, a fighting game which will involve some of the characters is being planned and worked on. He's appreciative of any criticism and advice on his work. Please feel free to contact us for more info, complaints, critiques and advice.
