The Comics
Dragmeria is a comic series centered on the monsters that reside on a world called Terrdivaeros. Where humanity the monsters they share the world with are in constant conflict. An old dragon, who once lived before the rift between humans and the rest of the inhabitants of Terrdivaeros deemed monsters by humanity, calls upon his grandchild to investigate a place deep within the land humans control to uncover why humanity and the world were thrown into conflict. Along with friends and allies gathered along the way, the ancient dragon's grandchild will seek out the truth, and possibly end the constant fighting.
